About Us

We are a children’s rights, welfare, and development organization based in Kenya with a vision to expand our reach across Africa. We acknowledge that no child chooses where they would like to be born and it is, therefore, our responsibility to ensure a safe bringing of children independent of their family background. We focus on all areas of abuse and neglect including physical, sexual, psychological, verbal, emotional, and mental abuse. We have a vibrant team of over 225 youth volunteers who donate their resources, skills, and time to improving the lives of children.
